Our Story

From Idea to Impact

Investnama was founded in 2023 with a simple yet powerful vision: to democratize financial knowledge and empower every Pakistani to take control of their financial future.

Today, only 400,000 Pakistanis have CDC accounts. We're on a mission to increase this to 10 million by making investment education accessible to everyone.

Our platform was born from the realization that financial literacy is the foundation of wealth creation. With "Know More. Grow More." as our guiding principle, we're building tools and resources that transform complex financial concepts into actionable knowledge.
